The Gateway to Kathiawad

Spiritual & Economic Heritage

Botad, the youngest district of Gujarat, is a unique blend of spiritual peace and industrial energy. Home to the world-renowned Salangpur Hanumanji Temple and Gadhada Swaminarayan Mandir, the district attracts millions of pilgrims, fostering a robust hospitality and service economy alongside its traditional roots in trade.

The Commercial Pulse

Known as the "White Gold" capital, Botad’s economy is anchored by massive cotton ginning and pressing units. Simultaneously, its small-scale diamond polishing units have become a vital part of Gujarat's jewelry supply chain. This diversity offers immense opportunities for skilled labor and industrial management professionals.
