Our Regional Legacy

A Hub of Global Trade

Bhavnagar holds a massive footprint in global maritime and industrial operations. Famous for the Alang Ship Breaking Yard—the largest of its kind in the world—it is a critical center for metal recycling and marine engineering.


Beyond the coastline, Bhavnagar drives major salt production, plastic manufacturing, and diamond cutting operations. This blend of heavy industry and specialized craftsmanship creates one of the most robust and diverse job markets in Gujarat.

Key Employment Sectors

Ship Breaking & Marine Engineering

Centered in Alang, this sector demands Marine Engineers, Safety Officers, Heavy Machinery Operators, and Scrap Logistics Managers.

Manufacturing & Salt Works

Bhavnagar is a leading producer of salt, plastics, and chemicals. Opportunities abound for Chemical Engineers, Plant Supervisors, and Quality Controllers.

Diamond Cutting & Polishing

A highly skilled sector offering jobs in Diamond Assaying, Precision Cutting, Export Management, and Artisanal Craftsmanship.
