Work History of Hooghly

Hooghly has historically been one of West Bengal’s major commercial and industrial districts. River trade, jute mills, agriculture, and manufacturing shaped the district’s employment landscape for decades.

Industrial Heritage

Hooghly became a strong industrial belt with jute mills, engineering factories, and river-based trade routes supporting thousands of jobs.

Agriculture & Rural Work

Farming, rice cultivation, and vegetable markets have remained important employment sources across rural Hooghly communities.
