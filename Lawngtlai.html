Economic Roots of Lawngtlai

A comprehensive reflection of Lawngtlai's historical livelihoods, centered on rich heritage crafts, traditional agriculture, and early baseline trades.

The Horticultural Landscape

Historically, the economic identity of Lawngtlai was tied entirely to its undulating agrarian hills. Shifting and terrace cultivation served as the backbone, with communities cultivating staple crops alongside extensive regional production of organic ginger, sesame, and native fruits.

Lai Artisans & Loom Heritages

Independent production relied heavily on local handlooms and woodcraft. Lai weavers generated iconic, distinct patterns that formed a strong barter-trade asset with neighboring valley sectors long before contemporary retail setups.
