Regional Story

Work History of Kangra District

Traditional Agriculture

Kangra has historically depended on farming, tea cultivation, and horticulture. The fertile valleys supported generations of livelihoods through traditional agricultural practices.

Artisans & Handicrafts

Local artisans contributed to Kangra’s cultural economy with handcrafted textiles, woodwork, and regional art forms that attracted tourism and trade.

Trade & Markets

Trade routes connected Kangra to nearby hill and plains regions, helping local businesses grow through tourism, handicrafts, and agriculture exports.

Tourism Growth

The rise of tourism in Dharamshala and surrounding areas transformed Kangra into a service-based employment hub with growing hospitality opportunities.

Employment Evolution

How Jobs in Kangra Have Evolved Over the Decades

Government Employment

Public administration, education departments, healthcare institutions, and infrastructure projects traditionally formed a large share of stable employment.

Private Sector Growth

Tourism, hospitality, cafés, logistics, and local retail businesses expanded rapidly due to increased visitors and urban development.

Modern Opportunities

Digital services, remote work, online education, and entrepreneurship are opening new possibilities for youth across Kangra district.
