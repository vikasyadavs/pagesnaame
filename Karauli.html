Work History of Karauli

Karauli, known for its rich heritage and historical sandstone architecture, has long been a land of skilled artisans and traditional agriculture. Historically, the local economy relied heavily on stone quarrying and crop cultivation, forming the foundation of regional trade.

Job History & Evolution

Over the decades, employment in Karauli has transitioned from purely agrarian and artisan-based labor toward a more diversified sector. The rise of small-scale industries and improved connectivity has shifted the focus toward service-oriented roles and government administration.
