The Development of Preet Vihar

An overview of Preet Vihar’s evolution into a key well-planned residential and bustling commercial neighborhood in East Delhi with prime transit connections.

Planned Residential Expansion

Developed in the late 20th century, Preet Vihar emerged as a premium, structured residential area in East Delhi, drawing corporate professionals, doctors, and commercial families due to its strategic proximity to central city zones.

The Blue Line Transit Revolution

The inauguration of the Delhi Metro Blue Line established Preet Vihar as a critical connection hub. The sudden high accessibility transformed Vikas Marg into a major commercial landmark for business towers and trade units.
