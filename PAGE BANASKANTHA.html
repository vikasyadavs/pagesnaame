Key Employment Sectors

Dairy & Food Processing

Banaskantha is home to Banas Dairy, Asia's largest milk producer. It drives massive employment across milk collection, processing, packaging, veterinary services, and logistics in Palanpur and beyond.

Agriculture & Agri-Tech

The district is the potato capital of Gujarat, especially around Deesa. This fuels significant job creation in cold storage management, agri-tech startups, irrigation engineering, and export operations.

Mining & Minerals

Banaskantha is rich in minerals, particularly marble and granite around Ambaji. The quarrying and stone processing sectors provide extensive opportunities for engineers, heavy machinery operators, and safety managers.
