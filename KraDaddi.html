The Kurung River basin offers immense energy potential. Opportunities for Civil Engineers, Environmental Scientists, and Power Plant Maintenance Staff.

With Jamin developing as the new district headquarters, there is a steady demand for Urban Planning experts, Surveyors, and Construction Project Managers.
