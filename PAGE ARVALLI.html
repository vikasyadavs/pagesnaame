Key Employment Sectors

Mining & Minerals

Arvalli is renowned for its rich mineral deposits, particularly granite and quartz. The quarrying and stone processing industries centered around Modasa and Malpur offer tremendous opportunities for engineers, logistics managers, and skilled technicians.

Agriculture & Agri-Tech

With fertile lands and a strong agrarian foundation, the district is a major producer of cotton, maize, and wheat. Opportunities are growing rapidly in agri-tech, supply chain management, and food processing.

Heritage Tourism

Home to the famous Shamlaji pilgrimage site and historic terrains, Arvalli is seeing steady growth in hospitality, cultural management, and local tourism infrastructure development.
