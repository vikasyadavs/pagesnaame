Economic Heritage of Kadapa

The Limestone & Slab Legacy

For centuries, Kadapa has been synonymous with its unique 'Kadapa Slabs'. The district's economy was built on the strength of limestone mining and the craftsmanship of stone-cutting artisans.

Agrarian Excellence

Famous for its sweet limes and turmeric, Kadapa’s trade history is deeply rooted in agriculture. Traditional farmers have adapted to modern irrigation, making it a regional hub for agro-trade.

Industrial Transformation

From a primarily agrarian society, Kadapa has transitioned into a key player in cement manufacturing and thermal power, attracting global investments in the Rayalaseema region.

Employment Evolution

The 1990s: Era of Artisans

Employment revolved around small-scale mining and traditional farming. Government jobs were the primary source of white-collar employment.

The 2010s: Industrial Surge

Large-scale cement plants (India Cements, Zuari) shifted the workforce from unorganized labor to organized industrial employment and engineering roles.

2025+: Green Energy & Tech

The focus has shifted towards solar power plants, tech-driven retail, and modern healthcare facilities, creating a demand for skilled professionals.
