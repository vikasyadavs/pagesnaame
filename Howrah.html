Work History of Howrah

Known as one of eastern India’s major industrial districts, Howrah has long been associated with engineering, foundries, rail connectivity, river trade, and manufacturing employment.

Industrial Backbone

Engineering workshops, foundries, and factories made Howrah one of the most important industrial employment hubs in West Bengal.

Transport & Railways
