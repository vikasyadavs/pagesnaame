A Legacy of Industry

From traditional craftsmanship to a modernizing economy, Markapuram has always been a hub of hard work.

The Slate Heritage

Known as the 'Slate City', Markapuram produces 80% of India's school slates. This industry has supported thousands of artisan families for generations.

Employment Evolution

The 90s focused on manual mining. Today, the district has shifted toward technology-driven manufacturing, retail, and professional services.
