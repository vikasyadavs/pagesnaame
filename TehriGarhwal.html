Historical Overview

The Structural & Geographic Transformation of Tehri Garhwal

The human resource trajectory of Tehri Garhwal is completely unique, reshaped by one of the largest engineering projects in the sub-continent. Historically acting as the sovereign royal capital of the Garhwal Kingdom, the older trade nodes sat deeply within the fertile river basins where the Bhagirathi met the Bhilangna.


With the structural execution of the massive Tehri Hydroelectric Dam, the entire core economy transitioned. The complete physical relocation of the population to the planned ridge town of New Tehri turned local vocational streams toward modern municipal engineering, systemic water sports operations, and structured hill-station hospitality services.
