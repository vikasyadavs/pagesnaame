The Heritage of Dahod & Regional Links

The Engineering Backbone

Dahod is deeply rooted in industrial engineering, best known for the massive Railway Locomotive Workshop established in 1912. This facility has historically employed thousands and continues to be a major driver for engineering and technical jobs in the region.

Tribal Craft & Smart City Growth

As a designated Smart City, Dahod is rapidly expanding its urban infrastructure. Concurrently, it remains a proud center for tribal heritage, creating a booming local economy around specialized crafts, agriculture, and smart city planning initiatives.
