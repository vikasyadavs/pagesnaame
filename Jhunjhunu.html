Work History of Jhunjhunu

Jhunjhunu, situated in the Shekhawati region, has a storied history of trade, banking, and military service. Historically, the community contributed significantly to national enterprise and defense, with a legacy built on the industrious spirit of its local artisan and merchant families.

Job History

Employment has transitioned from traditional agriculture and small-scale trade to a robust demand for technical skills, formal education, and modern entrepreneurship. Government employment remains a pillar, while the private sector is expanding in infrastructure and retail.
