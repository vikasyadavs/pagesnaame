The Spirit of Fatehpur

Fatehpur, nestled between the holy rivers Ganga and Yamuna, is a land of historical resilience. From its significant role in the 1857 freedom struggle to its emergence as an agricultural powerhouse, the district has always been a center of activity.


Historically, Fatehpur was known for its "Korah" (coarse cloth) production and traditional hand-block printing. Today, it serves as a vital trade link between Kanpur and Allahabad, offering unique opportunities in logistics and small-scale manufacturing.

Key Employment Sectors

Agro-Industries

With fertile land, there is a high demand for experts in food processing, cold storage management, and agricultural supply chains.

Handloom & Textiles

Revival projects in traditional weaving have created openings for designers, skilled artisans, and export managers.

Education & Services

As the district develops, the growth in private schools and coaching centers has increased the demand for educators and administrative staff.
