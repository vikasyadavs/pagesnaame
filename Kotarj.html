Work History of Kota

Kota has a rich heritage rooted in trade and agriculture along the banks of the Chambal River. Historically known for the Kota Doria weaving tradition and strategic trade routes, the local economy shifted dramatically in the late 20th century, emerging as a nationwide center for educational services and industrial manufacturing.

Job History & Evolution

Over the last four decades, Kota has evolved from a state industrial powerhouse to the "Education City" of India. The reliance on heavy industries has gradually shifted toward service-sector roles in education, hospitality, and modern retail, while maintaining strong manufacturing bases in its industrial corridors.
