Work History of Rajkot

Rajkot, the heart of Saurashtra, has always been an economic powerhouse. Historically known as the "Machine Tool Capital of India," its legacy is built on the sweat and grit of small-scale entrepreneurs and master artisans.

Traditional Roots

From the gold smithy of the Soni community to the intricate Bandhani textiles, Rajkot's trade history is centuries old, serving as a hub for the entire peninsula.

Employment Evolution

1980s-90s

Dominance of Diesel Engines & Casting industries. Rajkot supplied the nation's agricultural machinery.

2000s-10s

Expansion into Automobile parts and the rise of organized retail and healthcare sectors.

2025 Era

A hybrid market: High-tech manufacturing, thriving IT services, and a booming educational sector.
