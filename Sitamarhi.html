Work History of Sitamarhi

Sitamarhi has historically been known for agriculture, traditional trade, local craftsmanship, and regional commerce connecting Bihar and nearby Nepal border markets.

Traditional Farming

Agriculture has supported generations with crops like rice, wheat, maize, and sugarcane.

Regional Trade

Trade routes and local markets played a major role in creating employment.

Artisan Economy

Handicrafts, weaving, carpentry, and small workshops contributed to livelihoods.

Modern Opportunities

Education, retail, transport, and healthcare sectors are now expanding steadily.

Job History

Employment in Sitamarhi has evolved from government and agricultural work toward private-sector growth and digital opportunities.

1980s

Agriculture and public sector jobs dominated employment across the district.

2000s

Retail, transport, coaching centers, and banking sectors started growing rapidly.

Today

Healthcare, logistics, online services, and entrepreneurship create fresh opportunities.
