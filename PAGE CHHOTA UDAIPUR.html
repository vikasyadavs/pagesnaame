Our Regional Legacy

A Blend of Nature & Industry

Chhota Udaipur's economic foundation is deeply tied to its rich natural resources and vibrant tribal culture. Carved out of the Vadodara district, it has rapidly grown into a vital center for mineral extraction, particularly dolomite and fluorite.


Alongside industry, the region's vast forest cover provides crucial livelihood through agriculture and forestry. Today, the district is balancing industrial growth with educational and social development initiatives, creating a diverse job market.

Key Employment Sectors

Mining & Minerals

The largest private employment driver. Huge demand for Mining Engineers, Geologists, Heavy Machinery Operators, and Logistics Managers in the dolomite and quartz sectors.

Forestry & Agriculture

Leveraging the district's natural landscape. Growing opportunities for Agricultural Technicians, Forest Produce Supply Chain Managers, and Conservationists.

Education & Welfare

With a focus on tribal upliftment, there is continuous hiring by NGOs and government bodies for Rural Development Officers, Teachers, and Healthcare Coordinators.
