Work History of Namchi

Namchi, the headquarters of South Sikkim, has transitioned from a serene hill station to a thriving hub for religious tourism and administrative excellence. Its history is marked by a unique blend of traditional agricultural practices and a rapid surge in service-oriented industries supporting the local and regional tourism economy.

Job History

Employment trends in Namchi have moved significantly toward service sectors. While agriculture remains a vital traditional base, the last decade has seen a notable shift toward education, hospitality, and administrative roles, reflecting the region's development as a central node in Southern Sikkim.
