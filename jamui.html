History of Gaya

Economic Legacy of Gaya

Gaya is one of Bihar's most historic districts, famous for Bodh Gaya, spirituality, tourism and education. Over centuries, the district evolved from pilgrimage trade routes to a modern economy with tourism, railway and healthcare sectors.

Ancient Era

Gaya emerged as a sacred destination attracting pilgrims, monks and traders from across Asia. Religious tourism supported local businesses, artisans and accommodation services.

British Period

Railway expansion connected Gaya with Kolkata and Delhi, boosting trade, administration and formal employment opportunities.

Modern Gaya

Today Gaya thrives on tourism, education, railway services, healthcare, retail, digital services and infrastructure projects.
