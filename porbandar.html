Work History of Porbandar

Ancient Port Trade

Since the 16th century, Porbandar flourished as an important all-weather port, trading silk, cotton, and salt with Gulf countries.

Limestone & Cement

The 1900s marked the industrial revolution of the district with the discovery of high-quality limestone, leading to massive cement plants.

Fisheries Era

With its vast coastline, the mid-20th century saw the organized growth of the fishing and seafood processing industry.

Employment Evolution

Porbandar's employment landscape has transitioned from traditional maritime and salt-pan labor to high-tech industrial roles. While the 1980s were dominated by government-run textile and cement ventures, the last two decades have seen a massive shift toward private equity in chemicals, energy, and specialized hospitality.
