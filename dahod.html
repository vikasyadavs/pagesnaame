Work History of Dahod

The Gateway Tradition

Historically known as 'Dohad' (Two Boundaries), Dahod has been the strategic trade gateway between Gujarat, Rajasthan, and Madhya Pradesh for centuries.

Agricultural Roots

Traditionally an agrarian economy, Dahod is famous for its maize, pulse production, and the traditional tribal crafts that have supported local livelihoods.

Railway Industrialization

The establishment of the Railway Workshop during the British era marked the district's first major industrial shift, creating a culture of technical expertise.

Employment Evolution

The Industrial Dawn

Initially focused on the Railway Locomotive Workshop, providing steady government employment for thousands of families in the 20th century.

Small Scale Growth

The rise of GIDC estates and grain markets (APMC) led to a surge in retail, food processing, and small-scale manufacturing jobs.

Smart City Transformation

In 2025-26, the focus has shifted toward infrastructure, modern logistics, and smart-city services, opening doors for engineers and planners.
