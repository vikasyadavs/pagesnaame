Heritage Economy

Work History of Kullu

1960

Agriculture & Apple Farming

Kullu’s economy historically revolved around apple orchards, farming, wool weaving, and traditional Himalayan trade routes. Families relied heavily on seasonal agriculture and local craftsmanship.

1985

Tourism Expansion

Tourism transformed Kullu into a regional economic destination. Hotels, transport businesses, trekking agencies, and hospitality services expanded rapidly due to increasing domestic and international tourism.

2005

Modern Retail & Education

Educational institutions, retail businesses, and private enterprises created new employment opportunities for local youth while preserving Kullu’s cultural identity.

Employment Evolution

Job History in Kullu

1990

Government Sector Dominance

Government services, teaching jobs, tourism administration, and local public offices were the primary employment sources for educated youth.

2010

Private Hospitality Growth

Resorts, cafes, travel startups, and hospitality ventures accelerated hiring. Adventure tourism and luxury stays became major job creators.

2025

Digital & Hybrid Careers

Remote work, freelance design, social media marketing, eco-tourism ventures, and digital services are now creating premium opportunities across Kullu district.
