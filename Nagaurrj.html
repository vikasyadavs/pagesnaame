Nagaur has historically served as a strategic trade center in Rajasthan, known for its vibrant cattle fairs, handicraft traditions, and agricultural richness. From centuries-old art of tool-making to modern industrial initiatives, Nagaur's economy is a bridge between tradition and progress.
