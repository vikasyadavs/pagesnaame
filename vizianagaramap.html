Industrial & Economic Legacy

Understanding the district's journey from a royal seat to a modernizing industrial hub.

Royal Heritage

Established as a strategic kingdom, the district was historically famous for fine arts and its textiles trade under the Gajapati patronage.

The Jute Capital

For nearly a century, Vizianagaram was the 'Jute heart' of Andhra Pradesh, powering thousands of homes through massive processing mills.

Industrial Shift

The late 20th century saw a transition from traditional mills to ferro-alloys and pharmaceutical manufacturing units near the city.
